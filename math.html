Quick Revision Summary (Q1 - Q20 Block)

  • Key Formula: King's Rule: $\int_{0}^{a} f(x) dx = \int_{0}^{a} f(a-x) dx$. Always gives answer $\frac{\pi}{4}$ for symmetric trig fractions.
  • Key Formula: Shortest Distance (3D): $d = \frac{|(\vec{a}_2 - \vec{a}_1) \cdot (\vec{b}_1 \times \vec{b}_2)|}{|\vec{b}_1 \times \vec{b}_2|}$
  • Common Trap: In Matrix Method $AX = B$, students forget to multiply $\frac{1}{|A|}$ at the very end. Do NOT divide early to avoid fractions.
  • Most Scoring Area: LPP Graph plotting guarantees 2 marks even if corner points are calculated wrong.
